About Us

Zuwar Restaurant, located in Ramallah, is a popular dining establishment known for its diverse menu that showcases traditional Palestinian cuisine alongside international dishes. The restaurant offers a warm and inviting atmosphere, making it a favorite spot for both locals and tourists.

Highlights:

  • Cuisine: Zuwar Restaurant serves a variety of traditional Palestinian dishes, including mansaf (a lamb dish served with rice and yogurt sauce), maqluba (an upside-down rice and vegetable dish), and musakhan (chicken with onions, sumac, and olive oil on taboon bread). They also offer international options to cater to a wide range of tastes.

  • Ambiance: The restaurant is known for its comfortable and welcoming environment. It features traditional Palestinian decor, creating a cozy and authentic dining experience.

  • Location: Situated in the heart of Ramallah, Zuwar Restaurant is easily accessible and is a popular choice for family gatherings, business meetings, and casual dining.

  • Service: The staff at Zuwar Restaurant are noted for their hospitality and attentive service, ensuring a pleasant dining experience for all guests.

  • Events: Zuwar Restaurant also hosts special events and can accommodate large groups for celebrations and gatherings.
